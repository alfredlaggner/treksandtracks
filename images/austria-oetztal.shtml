Austria: Oetztal Adventure
Join us in the heart of the Alps!

This adventure is for skiers who want to experience authentic backcountry skiing in the heart of the Alps.

We do most of our skiing out of the town of Solden from where our local guides will take us to the most pristine and hidden gems in the region. We explore untoutched snow and explore the different glaciers and ski fields in Oetztal.

This is an opportunity to experience the chore culture of skiing in the Alps while learning essential backcountry ski and ski mountaineering skills from the best guides in the industry!

Space is limited, so reserve your spot!
