Rock n Stones Tour: England Scotland, Ireland

Book Now for an incredible journey of music and shamans in England, Scotland and Ireland

This unique journey combines music and the exploration of the shamanic world found in England, Ireland and Scotland. Visits to the Leeds music festival, standing stone circles, faerie glens and sacred springs are magically interwoven by your guide Jade Wah'oo Grigori. Jade, being a shaman of Mongolian heritage, has dedicated his life's work to guiding people into the realm of the Shaman.
